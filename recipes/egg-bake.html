Egg Bake

High protein

This egg bake is perfect for someone looking to hit their morning protein goals. It can be reheated in minutes and eaten at home or on the go. The base recipe is incredibly versatile so you can change around the ingredients to fit your preferences.

Ingredients
  • 8 eggs
  • 16 oz egg whites
  • 16 oz button mushrooms, chopped and lightly sautéd
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped and lightly sautéd
  • 1.5 cups cottage cheese
  • 1.5 cups peas, can be thawed from frozen
  • 3 bell peppers, any colors, chopped and lightly sautéd
  • 16 oz turkey bacon, or breakfast sausage of choice
  • 1 cup green onion, chopped
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1-2 tbs salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
Directions
  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees. Lightly oil a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a skillet, over medium heat, sauté your mushrooms, onion, and bell peppers until lightly browned. They do not need ot be fully cooked as they will continue to cook in the oven.
  3. Once your mixture is lightly browned, pour the mixture into your baking dish. Next, cook the turkey bacon or sausage in that same skillet according to package instructions.
  4. Once cooked, add that into the baking dish along with the peas and mix.
  5. In a blender, add in the eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, salt, pepper, and spinach. Blend until spinach is mixed in completely. Pour into the baking dish and mix.
  6. Place your baking sheet in the over for 40-55 minutes. If the top starts to brown, cover in aluminum foil.
  7. Once your egg mixture is cooked through, remove the dish from the oven and set aside for 10 minutes.
  8. Slice your creation into squares and sprinkle the green onion on top. Enjoy immediately or store in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 5 days.

Aside from the egg base mixture in the blender, any of these ingredients can be changed up!
